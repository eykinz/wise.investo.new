Simple Interest Rate Calculator

Calculate Future Value with Simple Interest

This calculator determines the future value of an investment or loan based on simple interest.

  • How to Use: Enter the initial investment amount, annual interest rate, and number of years.
  • Use For:To determine the total amount of money (future value) that will be accumulated from an initial investment or loan over a specified period at a given simple interest rate.
  • Calculate: The future value of the investment.

Calculate Present Value with Simple Interest

This calculator helps to find the present value or initial principal of an investment or loan when the future value, interest rate, and time period are known.

  • How to Use: Input the desired future value, annual interest rate, and number of years.
  • Use For: To find out how much you need to invest or loan today (present value) to reach a specific future value with simple interest.
  • Calculate: The present value required to achieve the future value.

Please enter valid values: Principal > 0, Time Period > 0, Simple Interest Rate > Principal.
